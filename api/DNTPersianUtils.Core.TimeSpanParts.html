ApproxDaysPerYear

The above are the average days per month/year over a normal 4 year period We use these approximations as they are more accurate for the next century or so After that you may want to switch over to these 400 year approximations ApproxDaysPerMonth = 30.436875 ApproxDaysPerYear = 365.2425 How to get these numbers: There are 365 days in a year, unless it is a leap year. Leap year is every forth year if Year % 4 = 0 unless year % 100 == 1 unless if year % 400 == 0 then it is a leap year. This gives us 97 leap years in 400 years. So 400 * 365 + 97 = 146097 days. 146097 / 400 = 365.2425 146097 / 400 / 12 = 30,436875 Due to the nature of the leap year calculation, on this side of the year 2100 you can assume every 4th year is a leap year and use the other approximatiotions
